    Hello ✨ I'm Clarise Santos, a Filipino-American college student and multimedia artist based in the Bay Area, California.

    I've tried all kinds of art mediums and classes over the years, but I recently settled into doing more digital work and art these days.

    My work tends to lean into the creative side of things as I'm willing to try different things to further improve my skills. As of writing, I'm in my junior year of college at San Jose State University and currently aiming to get into the Digital Media Art (DMA) program/degree by graduation.

    I've always had interests in anime/manga, journaling, or lounging around with a good RPG video game in my free time. My favorite JRPG is Persona 3 Reload and my favorite anime is "Yuru Camp". I've also recently played the "Atelier Ryza" video game triology.  

    I'd occasionally start new interests and update them in the "artwork" section of this website, though it's mostly experimental.
